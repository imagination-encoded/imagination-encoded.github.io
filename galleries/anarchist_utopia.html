The project uses images generated by Midjourney AI to explore collective unconscious and our ideas about the world.

The sole process of training a machine learning model is subject to unconscious bias. What data was available for training? What data was selected? The answers to these questions will not just define how the model works but also contain information about our society. Can we reconstruct the answers by looking at the images produced by the model?

I select a concept and create a prompt, an input, that describes it to the machine learning model. Midjourney generates the images. I welcome you to look at the images and reflect on what they can tell us about ourselves and how we view the world around us.

painting of an anarchist utopia"
